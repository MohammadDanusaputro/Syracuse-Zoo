Plan Your Visit

Our parks are Living Classrooms where learning comes to life. Enrich your curriculum with a free-and-easy visit or take up an education programme for a multisensory learning experience! Plan a meaningful trip for your students with the information below.

School Admission Rates

The following admission rates apply to the listed academic institutions. School groups must pre-register with the Education Department to be granted school admission rates.

For Students
Primary & Below

Local kindergartens, children centers, Primary schools, before and after school care centers, locally-operating international schools.

Secondary School & Up

Local Secondary Schools, Junior Colleges, Centralized Institutes, ITEs, Polytechnics & Universities & Locally-Operating international schools.
